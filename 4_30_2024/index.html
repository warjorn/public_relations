Speedblox, acquisition on May 1, 2024:

Following the acquisition of Speedblox Racing on May 1, 2024, we are thrilled to embark on an exciting journey of expansion and enhancement. As we integrate Speedblox Racing into our portfolio, our primary focus will be on increasing accessibility and refining the gaming experience to align with the highest standards of quality.

Currently, in its beta testing phase, access to Speedblox Racing is limited to a select group of developers and quality assurance team members. This exclusivity enables us to meticulously identify and address any issues within the game, ensuring it meets and exceeds the stringent standards set by WARJORN.

Speedblox Racing boasts several key features that set it apart. Built on the robust ROBLOX platform, the game offers seamless data-saving functionality, providing players with a smooth and uninterrupted gaming experience. Furthermore, its user-friendly interface facilitates easy editing, allowing for continuous updates and improvements.

One of the key revenue streams within Speedblox Racing is the availability of game passes, offering players additional content and features in exchange for a fee. This not only enhances the gameplay experience but also contributes to the sustainability and growth of the game.

Moreover, Speedblox Racing introduces a unique element with customizable vehicles, allowing players to personalize their gaming experience and express their creativity. Whether it's tweaking performance attributes or enhancing aesthetics, the customization options are virtually limitless, adding depth and personalization to the gameplay.

In addition to these features, Speedblox Racing offers a battle pass system that emphasizes cosmetic enhancements rather than providing gameplay advantages. This ensures a level playing field for all players while allowing them to showcase their progression and achievements through visually striking additions to their gaming experience.

As we move forward with the acquisition of Speedblox Racing, we are committed to elevating the game to new heights of excellence, delivering an unparalleled gaming experience that captivates and delights players around the globe. Stay tuned for more updates as we unveil our plans for the future of Speedblox Racing under the banner of WARJORN.

Levelup Advertising:


We are thrilled to unveil the culmination of our partnership as we officially integrate Levelup Advertising as a key subsidiary within our ecosystem. This milestone marks a significant step forward for us, particularly in enhancing the immersive experience of our flagship product, "Speedblox Racing."

With the incorporation of Levelup Advertising's resources, "Speedblox Racing" is poised to revolutionize the gaming landscape by introducing a groundbreaking feature: player-driven advertisements. This innovative approach empowers gamers to seamlessly promote their content within the game environment, fostering a dynamic and engaging community.

Our primary objective is to streamline the advertising process, ensuring it is effortlessly accessible with just a simple click. Moreover, we are committed to preserving the integrity of the gaming experience, safeguarding against intrusive interruptions. To achieve this, we have strategically designated the lobby as the exclusive platform for advertisements, and strategically placed non-invasive elements such as portals or tasteful banners adorning virtual buildings.

By prioritizing user convenience and maintaining a harmonious balance between gameplay and promotional content, we are poised to redefine the boundaries of interactive entertainment. Together with Levelup Advertising, we look forward to charting new frontiers and delivering unparalleled experiences for our dedicated gaming community.
